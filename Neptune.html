Neptune

Ice Giant

Orbital Characteristics:

  • Distance from the Sun: 4.5 billion km (30.07 AU)
  • Orbital Period: 165 Earth years
  • Rotation Period: Approximately 16 hours

Physical Characteristics:

  • Diameter: 49,244 km
  • Surface Gravity: 11.15 m/s²
  • Surface Composition: Similar to Uranus, primarily composed of ices and rock.

Atmospheric Composition:

  • Mainly hydrogen, helium, and methane, which also contributes to its blue color.

    • Temperature:

    • Average temperature around -214°C (-353°F).

    Scientific Interest:

    • Neptune's dynamic atmosphere, featuring the strongest winds in the solar system, and its intriguing moon system, including Triton, which shows evidence of cryovolcanism, are key areas of study.
