Mars

Type: Terrestrial Planet

Orbital Characteristics:

  • Distance from the Sun: 227.9 million km (1.52 AU)
  • Orbital Period: 687 Earth days
  • Rotation Period: 243 Earth days (retrograde rotation)

Physical Characteristics:

  • Diameter: 6,779 km
  • Surface Gravity: 3.71 m/s²
  • Surface Composition: Composed primarily of iron oxide, giving it a reddish color; features include volcanoes, valleys, and polar ice caps.

Atmospheric Composition: Thin atmosphere, approximately 95% carbon dioxide, with nitrogen and argon.

Average surface temperature around -63°C (-81°F), with significant seasonal variations.

Mars is a primary target for astrobiological studies and future human exploration due to evidence of past water.
