Saturn

Type: Gas Giant

Orbital Characteristics:

  • Distance from the Sun: 1.43 billion km (9.58 AU)
  • Orbital Period: 29.46 Earth years
  • Rotation Period: Approximately 10.7 hours

Physical Characteristics:

  • Diameter: 116,460 km
  • Surface Gravity: 10.44 m/s²
  • Surface Composition: Mostly hydrogen and helium, with a potential rocky core surrounded by metallic hydrogen.

Atmospheric Composition:

  • Similar to Jupiter, primarily hydrogen and helium with traces of ammonia and methane.

Temperature:

  • Average temperature around -178°C (-288°F).

Scientific Interest

  • Saturn’s extensive ring system and numerous moons, particularly Titan, with its dense atmosphere and liquid methane lakes, make it a focus of astrobiological research.
