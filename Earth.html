Earth

Type: Terrestrial Planet

Orbital Characteristics:
  • Distance from the Sun: 149.6 million km (1 AU)
  • Orbital Period: 365.25 days
  • Rotation Period: 24 hours

Physical Characteristics:

  • Diameter: 12,742 km
  • Surface Gravity: 9.81 m/s²
  • Surface Composition: Diverse environments including oceans, mountains, deserts, and ice caps.

Atmospheric Composition:

  • Composed of 78% nitrogen, 21% oxygen, with trace gases including argon, carbon dioxide, and water vapor.

Temperature Range

  • Average global temperature of approximately 15°C (59°F), with regional variations.

Scientific Interest:

  • As the only known planet to support life, Earth is crucial for studying biospheric processes and climate dynamics.
