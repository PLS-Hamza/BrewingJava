Jupiter

Type: Gas Giant

Orbital Characteristics:

  • Distance from the Sun: 778.5 million km (5.20 AU)
  • Orbital Period: 11.86 Earth years
  • Rotation Period: Approximately 9.9 hours

Physical Characteristics:

  • Diameter: 139,820 km
  • Surface Gravity: 24.79 m/s²
  • Surface Composition: Primarily hydrogen and helium, with a possible rocky core.

Atmospheric Composition:

  • Dominated by hydrogen (about 90%) and helium (about 10%), with traces of ammonia, methane, and water vapor.

Temperature:

  • Average temperature around -145°C (-234°F) in the upper atmosphere.

Scientific Interest

  • Jupiter’s strong magnetic field, complex weather systems, and extensive moon system (including Europa, which may harbor subsurface oceans) are of significant interest in planetary science.
