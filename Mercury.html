Mercury

Terrestrial Planet

Orbital Characteristics:

  • Distance from the Sun: 57.91 million km (0.39 AU)
  • Orbital Period: 88 Earth days
  • Rotation Period: 58.65 Earth days (3:2 spin-orbit resonance)

Physical Characteristics:

  • Diameter: 4,880 km
  • Surface Gravity: 3.7 m/s²
  • Surface Composition: Primarily silicate rock and metals; features impact craters, smooth plains, and scarps.

Atmospheric Composition:

  • Very tenuous, composed mainly of oxygen, sodium, hydrogen, helium, and potassium.

Temperature Range:

  • Daytime temperatures can reach up to 430°C (800°F), while nighttime temperatures can drop to -180°C (-290°F).

Scientific Interest::

  • Mercury's extreme temperature variations and geological features make it a point of interest for studying planetary formation and evolution.
